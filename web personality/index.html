About Us

We are a group of 3 people that needed to do something for people who were bored during quarantine because of the COVID-19 virus.

We made this site for people who don't want/can't afford to pay to watch movies/series.

Movies 4 U is supposed to be a free streaming service, we have no intentions to monetize our service.

Click the "Prototype" Button in the navigation bar to have a sneak peek on how a visitor would experience it, or scroll down further to see what we have in store for you!

If you have any related questions to our project or want to keep up with the latest updates, feel free to contact us or sign up for the newsletter down below!
